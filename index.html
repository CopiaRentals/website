Our Mission

We help colleges maximize resources by renting out unused dorms to trusted tenants during off-peak seasons, providing affordable short-term housing while supporting institutional budgets.

Case Study: River Trail Village

We partnered with River Trail Village, a 588-bed, three-building apartment complex on the campus of Napa Valley College in Napa, CA. Copia Rentals has launched an omnichannel marketing effort, increasing leasing activity and supporting student housing growth.
